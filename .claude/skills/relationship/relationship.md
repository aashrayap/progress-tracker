# Relationship Log

Append-only thought records. Each entry follows CBT structure: trigger → thought → feeling → state → distortion → evidence → reframe.

### 2026-03-03 (retrospective — from clarity check-in)

- **Trigger:** Relationship clarity conversation with self
- **Thought:** "I love her but last 3 visits I wanted to break up — am I convincing myself to love her?"
- **Feeling:** confusion, fear, guilt
- **State:** rested, clean, evening, solo reflection
- **Distortion:** ROCD spiral + scarcity fear (both present)
- **Evidence for:** 3 visits wanting to break up is a pattern, not a one-off. Enjoyment answer is "unsure" not "yes."
- **Evidence against:** Attraction, respect, shared goals all solid. Being yourself = yes. May be confusing LDR friction with relationship quality.
- **Reframe:** "The doubt is real and worth tracking, but 3 high-stress visits in an LDR aren't the same as daily life together. I don't have enough clean data yet."
