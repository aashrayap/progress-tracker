### The Anti-Goals

1. **I refuse to be someone who numbs instead of acts.** Not just the substances — the pattern of masking problems with weed, junk food, or screens instead of facing them through healthy means.

2. **I refuse to be isolated in my room in 1, 3, or 5 years.** Working from home, no local friends, no weekend plans to look forward to. As a child, weekends meant excitement. I want that back.

3. **I refuse to have money but no relationships.** The Harvard study: at the end of life, people looked back on relationships, not net worth. I'm terrified of ending up on the wrong side of that.

4. **I refuse to look back and feel trapped by missed opportunity.** The fear of waking up older and realizing I didn't become the best person I could have been when I had the chance.

5. **I refuse to keep knowing what's wrong without changing.** Self-awareness without action is just a more sophisticated form of avoidance.
