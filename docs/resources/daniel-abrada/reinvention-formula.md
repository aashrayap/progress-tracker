# The Reinvention Formula — Daniel Abrada

How to become unrecognizable in 12 months.

## 5-Step System
1. **Define Your North Star** — ABT formula (Actual → Becoming → Timeline) across 4 domains: Health, Wealth, Love, Self. Be brutally specific and measurable.
2. **Construct a New Identity** — Write a detailed character sheet of who you're becoming. Read it daily. Also write anti-vision (who you refuse to be). Identity is decided, built, lived, reinforced.
3. **Control Your Daily Inputs** — Full spectrum audit (mental, emotional, physical, digital). If it's not helping, it's hurting. Mentors, books, podcasts, documentaries, music — all curated. Purge everything misaligned.
4. **Remove Internal Distractions** — Inventory everything that pulls attention. Disable, block, remove. Replace with anchors (morning intention, deep work blocks, evening shutdown).
5. **Install Powerful Habits** — ABT becomes ABTH (H = Habits). Morning: direction. Midday: reset anchors. Evening: reflection + tomorrow prep. Track everything. Miss once, not twice. Quarterly review and upgrade.

## Key Concepts
- Identity is installed through ritual and reinforcement, not intention
- Your brain believes what's repeated, not what's true
- Habits confirm identity — every action is a vote for who you believe you are
- Read identity script every morning out loud
- Habit stacking: link new behaviors to existing triggers
- Progress is a feeling you can construct — momentum keeps you going
